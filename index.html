Word Finding APP

Instructions:

  • In First step You pick a word in your Mind and enter the length of it.
  • In Second step you will be prompt with 2d display of letters based on which you have to enter the column number that the letters of your word fall in, one by one.
  • In third step the second step will be repeated.
  • Finally you need to click the find Button and the app will find the word you picked.
